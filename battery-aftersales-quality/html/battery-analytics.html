    Aftersales QC takeaway

    • Log IR per module and compute spread % (weak vs strong) at intake and after repair.
    • Verify BMS balancing reduces ΔV during test charge — not only “comm OK”.
    • Trend spread over cycles on repeat customers — widening spread predicts efficiency loss before catastrophic fail.
    • Do not extrapolate 1:1 rules (1% spread = 1% eff.) — use ranges and C-rate context from literature.
