Before repair → after the 8 gates

A returned pack often looks fine on the outside. The gate sequence turns symptoms into verified release.

Before (typical return)

  • Customer report: “BMS timeout” or weak range
  • Unknown prior repairs or wrong firmware
  • Pressure to ship in 4 days
  • Risk: swap parts until something works
→

After (gate release)

  • IR pass · balance OK · CAN alive
  • Root cause logged (not symptom-only fix)
  • FTTR Yes · traceable torque & parts
  • Fleet risk flagged if batch-related
